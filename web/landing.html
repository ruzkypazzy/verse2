How it works

  1. 1

    Verse2 listens

    It downloads your audio and runs spectral clustering to detect the song's structure — intro, verse, chorus, bridge, outro.

  2. 2

    It writes the treatment

    An LLM produces three distinct visual concepts: cinematic logline, visual style, scene-by-scene shot list, and pacing notes.

  3. 3

    It prices the shoot

    A deterministic budget engine tallies locations, cast, dancers, camera, lighting, drone, and FX against a versioned rate card. If you're over cap, it iterates 6 strategies to fit.

Under the hood

  • Audio analysis · Python sidecar (FastAPI + librosa), Laplacian segmentation with novelty-based structural clustering
  • Visual concepts · gpt-4o-mini (OpenAI-compatible), prompt-engineered for cinematic specificity
  • Budget engine · deterministic, 4 rate cards (NGN, USD, EUR, GBP), 6 optimization strategies
  • Payments · x402 v2 protocol, CAIP-2 eip155:196, USDT0 contract 0x779d...3736
  • Storage · better-sqlite3 with WAL mode, jobs persist across requests
  • Deploy · single Docker image (Node + Python sidecar), 3-minute cold start on Railway
